Hi, I'm @karelvuong.

And I am a digital artisan. I design things, but I don't see design as pushing pixels around a screen. Taking a broad definition of design, I think of it as finding something that is broken and fixing it.

Design starts with empathy — feeling people's pain. This leads me to try and find flaws in the system that created this pain and fix them. Whether it is through designing a solution to a complex algorithm, architecting an application from start to finish, or making something easier on the eyes, designing to improve and solve problems is what I do.
