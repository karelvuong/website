My Interests

I am fascinated with the concept of openness. This speaks to several open movements, but more specifically, open data and open source initiatives. With regards to open data, over my short lifetime, I have already seen a shift on multiple fronts. At the government level, open data increases both transparency and accountability. Even more exciting is the enabling of third parties to leverage this data to develop applications and services that can now address public and private demands.

I am also captivated by wearable technology. It was said by Forbes that 2014 will be the year of wearable tech. I look forward to the day where I will be able to fully quantify every aspect of my life. Statistics galore!
